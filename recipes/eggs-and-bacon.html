EGGS AND BACON

Description

The combination that is bacon-and-eggs is unrivaled in the category of Best Basic Breakfasts. Here's a quick how-to that will satisfy the morning munchies, as well as (hopefully) keep you in the good graces of any resident fire demons.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar

Steps

  1. Heat pan and add bacon, letting the bacon fat act as grease. Cook until desired cripiness.
  2. Chop onion into thin slices, and heat oil in a large skillet on medium-high until sizzling. Add onions, and cook until clear and soft.
  3. Season onions with thyme and salt and let cook. Add red wine and vinegar and cook for about another minute.
  4. In the same pan as the onions, add eggs, and cook 2-3 minutes or until crispy on the bottom and the white is cooked.
  5. Remove eggs and onion from pan, and serve with bacon.
